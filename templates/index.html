To predict the probabilitites of match results in a particular match, you will have to provide 3 numbers:

Home team win %: This is number is calculated by taking the number of wins in this season divided by total matches played. You will find these two numbers in any official Premier Leauge table on the web

Away team win %: Same procedure as above - but now you do the same calulation for the Away team

Historical Home wins: This is the number of times the Home team has won in previous meetings between the two teams you are predicting. This number is usually pre-calculated and displayed in the "Statistics" section for the match on common betting odds websites

Pleasae note that you can only sumbit whole numbers - any decimal number has to be rounded up or down

Input example: Home team win %: 38 | Away team win %: 34 | Historical Home wins: 40 (you only put in the numbers)
