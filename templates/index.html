LIFA Premier League MatchPredictor

Welcome to Premier League MatchPredictor 1.0 - by Lambertseter Institute of Fotball Analysis ! This is a Support Vector Machine classifier with 65-70% accuracy and it is a stripped down and simplified version of LIFAs main model - TippekupongenGenerator 2.0

Please note that this website is for showcase purposes only. LIFA takes no responsibilities for any losses occuring from odds betting based on the predictions from this model. Last match day included in model data: 01.03.2020

To predict the most likely match result, you will have to provide 3 historical head-to-head numbers: HomeTeam Win %, Draw % and AwayTeam Win %

You will have to do three simple caluclations and put the numbers you get into the schema at the bottom of this page.

You can find the numbers you need to do the calculations - here: Premier League head-to-head statistics.

On the Premier Leauge statistcs page - select HomeTeam and AwayTeam to see historical Wins, Draws and Losses for the two teams. Remember to use the "Current" option (not "All-time")

Then you do some simple math - divide HomeTeam wins by total matches Played, Draws by total matches Played and finally AwayTeam wins by total matches Played.

Round the three numbers you get to the nearest whole number (no decimals) - put them into the schema and press "Predict"
